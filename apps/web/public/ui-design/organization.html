About

GenAI Builders is a grassroots developer community founded in Shenzhen in 2023. We believe in learning by building — every month we organize hackathons where participants ship real products in 48 hours.

Our members have collectively launched 50+ projects, raised $2M+ in funding, and built tools used by thousands. Whether you're a seasoned engineer or just getting started with AI, you'll find your tribe here.
