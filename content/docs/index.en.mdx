Hackathon = Hack + Marathon, an activity format for rapidly developing product prototypes in a concentrated timeframe. In our community:
- **"Weekly"**: Opportunities to participate in creation every week
- **"Hackathon"**: Efficient collaboration in short time to turn ideas into reality
